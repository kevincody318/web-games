10 Popular Web Games

  • 1. Among Us - A multiplayer game of teamwork and betrayal.
  • 2. Fortnite - A game that combines building and battle royale elements.
  • 3. League of Legends - A popular MOBA game with strategic gameplay.
  • 4. Minecraft - A sandbox game that allows players to build and explore.
  • 5. Call of Duty: Warzone - A battle royale game set in the Call of Duty universe.
  • 6. Roblox - A platform that allows users to create and play games made by other users.
  • 7. Hearthstone - A digital collectible card game set in the Warcraft universe.
  • 8. Counter-Strike: Global Offensive - A team-based first-person shooter game.
  • 9. Genshin Impact - An open-world action role-playing game.
  • 10. Dota 2 - Another popular MOBA that has a large esports scene.
